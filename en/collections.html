Collections

Collection — Cercle Premier

Composition

6 dinner plates 27 cm · 6 wide soup plates 22–23 cm · 6 starter/dessert plates 20–21 cm

Ivory porcelain, dense and deep. A balance between matte surface and subtle gloss. A discreet central engraving. A continuous circle, barely perceptible. A single blue line. Identifiable. Never assertive. This collection is designed to be read closely. From a distance, it almost disappears. It is not made to impress. It is made to be recognized.
