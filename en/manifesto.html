Manifesto

Porcelaine was born from the desire to slow down. We believe that simple gestures—preparing tea, serving a meal—deserve objects designed to last.

Our pieces are shaped by hand in dense, luminous porcelain. Each collection carries a unique imprint: a glaze nuance, the curve of a rim, the warmth of a texture.

We work with partner ateliers to keep production responsible, reduce waste, and pass on rare craftsmanship.

Choosing Porcelaine means welcoming a gentler rhythm at home, attentive to the beauty of details.
