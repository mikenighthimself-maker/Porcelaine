Reception set, 18 pieces

Porcelain, royal blue and gold line

Contemporary period

Conceived as a closed ensemble, this reception set is composed of eighteen pieces intended for six guests. Only three forms: a flat plate, a wide soup plate, an intermediate serving plate. The porcelain, a deep royal blue, absorbs light before returning it with restraint. A fine gold line marks the contour; at the center, a discreet seal affirms presence without comment. The set is complete. The remainder belongs to the gesture, the dish, the host.
